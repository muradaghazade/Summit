A BLAST FROM PAST

Since starting out in 2021, Annual Innovation Summit has grown to become “one of the biggest and best innovation conferences in Azerbaijan”.

OUR
HISTORY

Originally, Canvas started out as a developer conference, it was an opportunity to grow a community of like-minded digital doers and discuss what people in our industry were working on, how they were building it, and what insights and techniques they could share.

Each year, we bring together a collection of voices that offer new ways to approach creating digital products and experiences. Since our first event over 10 years ago, we’ve heard insider stories from 84 speakers spanning 79 different brands and welcomed over 3,000 product people to Birmingham for a day of content, networking and development!
