Energy Vertical

Global Innovation Trends in Energy Sector: Fossils and Renewables

Jeroen van der Veer

Former CEO of Shell, Vice-Chair of Equinor and governing Board EIT (European Institute for Technology and Innovation)

Short- and long-term innovation trends in the supply side of energy both in fossils and Renewables. Covering electrification, nuclear and CCUS (Carbon use / storage), trends in the demand side including smart grids, consumer behaviours and what governments can do regarding the energy mix, the role of Carbon pricing and Carbon border tasks, touching the finance side of the energy transition and longer-term geopolitical aspects, ending with his views regarding the role/opportunities for startups/scale ups on the one hand and the traditional big energy companies on the other hand.
